## Development History

The IntentKernel architecture was developed through iterative design sessions beginning in mid-2025. The conceptual lineage traces through three naming iterations — Lumen (mobile-focused), QShield OS (hardware-integrated), and IntentKernel (universal substrate) — each refining the core insight that ambient authority is the root cause of all computing security failures.

AI language models (OpenAI ChatGPT, Anthropic Claude) were used as collaborative design partners during architecture development. All architectural decisions, design philosophy, and strategic direction are the work of Daniel Kirk Owings.
